About IFF

The Fraunhofer Institute for Factory Operation and Automation(Fraunhofer IFF) is the part of the huge organization Fraunhofer-Gesellschaft for applied research and development in the field of Industrial Engineering and Business Informatics. It Researches and develops practical user inclined research in the sectors of virtual Engineering, Logistics and Material flow Technology, Automation and Plant Engineering.

BIM
